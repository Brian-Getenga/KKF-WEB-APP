Our Mission

To promote and develop karate as a sport and martial art throughout Kenya, fostering discipline, respect, and excellence in every practitioner.

Our Vision

To be the leading martial arts organization in Africa, producing world-class athletes and contributing to national development through sport.

Our Story

Founded in 1995, the Kenya Karate Federation has grown from a small group of dedicated practitioners to become the official governing body for karate in Kenya.

Our journey began with a vision to bring traditional Japanese karate to Kenya while respecting our rich cultural heritage. Over the past three decades, we have trained thousands of students, from beginners taking their first steps on the mat to Olympic-level athletes representing Kenya on the world stage.

As an affiliate of the World Karate Federation (WKF) and a proud member of the Olympic Council of Kenya (NOCK), we uphold the highest standards in martial arts education. Our instructors are certified black belts with international credentials, ensuring that every student receives authentic, world-class training.

Beyond competition and physical training, we emphasize the philosophical aspects of karate - discipline, respect, perseverance, and the continuous pursuit of self-improvement. These values extend beyond the dojo, shaping our students into confident, respectful members of society.

"Karate is not about winning or losing, but about the perfection of character." - Gichin Funakoshi
