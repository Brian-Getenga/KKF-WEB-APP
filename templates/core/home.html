Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining KKF

What age can my child start karate?

Children can start training from age 5. Our kids program is specifically designed for ages 5-12, focusing on coordination, discipline, and fun learning.

Do I need prior experience to join?

No experience is required! We welcome complete beginners and have classes for all skill levels from white belt to advanced black belts.

What equipment do I need?

For your first class, comfortable athletic wear is fine. After joining, you'll need an official KKF gi (uniform) which can be purchased from our shop.

How long does it take to get a black belt?

With consistent training (3-4 times per week), most students achieve black belt in 4-6 years. The journey varies based on dedication, attendance, and individual progress.

Are the certifications internationally recognized?

Yes! Kenya Karate Federation is affiliated with the World Karate Federation (WKF) and the Africa Karate Federation. All our certifications are internationally recognized.
