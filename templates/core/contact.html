Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly

What are your class schedules?

We offer classes from Monday to Sunday. Morning classes start at 6:00 AM, afternoon at 2:00 PM, and evening at 6:00 PM. Weekend classes are available on Saturday and Sunday mornings. Visit our schedule page for detailed timings.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes! We offer one free trial class for all new students. This allows you to experience our teaching methods and facility before committing to a membership. Book your trial class through our website or call us directly.

What age groups do you cater to?

We have programs for everyone! Kids (ages 5-12), Teens (13-17), and Adults (18+). Each age group has specially designed curriculum to match their developmental stage and learning needs.

What should I bring to my first class?

For your first class, just bring comfortable athletic wear and a water bottle. We provide temporary gi (uniform) for trial classes. After joining, you'll need to purchase an official KKF gi from our pro shop.
